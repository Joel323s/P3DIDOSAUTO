# Vendor Cockpit: A High-Fidelity Business Dashboard

Welcome to your revamped Vendor Dashboard, designed as a modern "Cockpit" for your business. This document explains the features and how to leverage them.

## 1. The Header & Stats Panel (Cockpit)
- **Top Metrics**: View real-time data at a glance:
  - **Total Ventas**: Today's sales (USD).
  - **Productos Activos**: Number of available items.
  - **Agotados**: Items with zero stock.
  - **Tasa Cambio (ARS/$):** Current exchange rate.

## 2. Inventario (Inventory) - "Control Command"
- **Product Grid**: View all items as sleek cards.
  - **Stock Status**: Automatically highlighted (Green = Available, Red = Agotado/Out).
  - **Price Glance**: See USD and calculated ARS prices instantly.
  - **Actions**: Edit or Delete products directly from the card.
- **Add/Edit Form**: Click "+ Nuevo Producto" to open a robust, dark-themed form.
  - **Pricing**: Set USD, ARS, and Bolivianos prices. Use "Vender por Docena" for special pricing.
  - **Stock**: Manage distinct stock units (Units vs Dozens).
  - **Images**: Upload multiple images with a drag-and-drop interface.
